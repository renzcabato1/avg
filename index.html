    Company Background

    A.G.V Customs Brokerage is a Sole Proprietorship Business consist of Professionals which is highly experienced on handling Importation and Exportation of Shipments and other services but not limited to Domestic Freight Forwarding, International Freight Forwarding and Trucking services.

    As basic consideration to maintain our well-organized management, our company is committed to:

    • Comply with all the relevant regulatory requirements pertaining to the quality of service.
    • Provide high-quality, affordable contract of services solutions.
    • Create and cultivate long-term relationships with clients.
    • Respond immediately to the changing needs of our clients.
    • Achieve complete customer satisfaction.
    • Improve our services continuously.

    A.G.V Customs Brokerage is committed to these objectives by continuously motivating and supporting its Team Members and conducting strategic planning for continuous development in our operational activities.

    Our Pride

    A.G.V Customs Brokerage and its members are composed of a licensed Customs Broker accredited with the Bureau of Customs and other professionals with proven managerial and supervision skills acquired on our previous employment for almost 10 years. We are able to start this company from the experiences we had on our previous employment and considered this company as a fruit of our labor.
